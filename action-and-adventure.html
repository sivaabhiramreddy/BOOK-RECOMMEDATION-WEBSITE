Realm Breaker

Realm Breaker is more of a Fellowship story than a Chosen One story and follows a group of unexpected allies who must work together to save their universe from a seemingly insurmountable evil force. It's difficult to condense the intricate world-building plot, but it's Tolkienesque in nature.
